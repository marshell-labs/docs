chifu runs a stack of security scanners against your sites and rolls
the output into a single **risk score** with explainable
**findings**. You add a domain, verify ownership, and click **Scan** —
no scripts, no config files.

## What you get

- A risk score from 1 to 99, broken down into five components.
- Findings with severity, evidence, and a remediation note.
- A reconnaissance snapshot: tech stack, open ports, forms, subdomains,
  CDN/WAF.
- Optional scheduled scans (daily / weekly / monthly) and firewall
  bypass for sites behind Vercel or Cloudflare bot protection.
