---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "First scan in under five minutes."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create an account at [marshell.dev/register](https://marshell.dev/register).

    You're placed on the **Free** plan (3 scans / month) and a one-time
    API key is shown as an amber banner on **Settings → API &
    Webhooks**. Copy it now — chifu won't show it again.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a domain">
    Go to **Domains → Add domain** and enter a hostname
    (`example.com`). Status will be **Pending** until you verify
    ownership.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify ownership">
    Expand the domain card and pick one method:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="DNS TXT">
        Add a TXT record at `_chifu.{your-domain}` with the value
        chifu shows. Click **Verify** after DNS propagates.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="File">
        Serve `chifu-token.txt` (containing only the token) at
        `https://{your-domain}/chifu-token.txt`. On Next.js / Vercel,
        place it in `/public`. Click **Verify**.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run a scan">
    On **Scans → Run scan now**, pick the verified domain and click
    **Scan**. The pipeline takes about 8 minutes. Status in **Scan
    history** updates live: `Queued → Running → Completed`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read the result">
    Click the scan row. You'll see:

    - **Risk gauge** — score 1..99 with a color band.
    - **Score breakdown** — the five components and what hurt them.
    - **Tech stack**, **Known vulnerabilities**, **Other findings**.

    Details: [Risk score](/risk-score), [Findings](/findings).
  </Step>
</Steps>
