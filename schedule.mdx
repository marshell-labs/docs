<Warning>
  Scheduling is a **Pro** feature. On Free, the page is read-only with
  an upgrade overlay. See [Plans](/plans).
</Warning>

The **Schedule** page is a 7-day × 24-hour grid. You drop blocks
onto cells; each block is a recurring scan.

## Add a block

Hover an empty cell to reveal a `+`. Click it — a block appears with
your first verified domain, set to **weekly** (same weekday, same
hour, every week).

## Configure a block

Click the block to open a popover:

- **Domain** — pick from your verified domains.
- **Run now** — triggers a scan immediately, doesn't change the
  schedule.
- **Pause / Resume** — temporarily disables the block without
  deleting it.
- **Remove** — deletes the schedule.

Drag the block to a different cell to change day/hour.

## Frequency

You can also set explicit frequency from **Scans → Schedule scan**:

| Frequency | Fires |
|---|---|
| Once | Once at a date + time |
| Daily | Every day at the chosen hour |
| Weekly | A chosen weekday + hour |
| Monthly | A chosen day of month + hour |

## Quotas

| Plan | Scans / month | Scans / day |
|---|---|---|
| Free | 3 | — (scheduling locked) |
| Pro | 30 | 1 |
| Max | 120 | 4 |

When the monthly quota runs out, scheduled scans are skipped
(`quota exhausted`) until the next month. Blocks stay on the
calendar; they start firing again on the 1st.
