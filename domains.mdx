Before chifu scans a site, you verify that you own it. This keeps everyone honest:
you can only scan domains you control. Manage them on the **Domains** page.

## Add a domain

Click **Add domain** and enter your hostname, like `myapp.com` or `myapp.vercel.app`.
It appears with a **Pending** badge until you verify it.

<Note>
  Enter a bare hostname, no `https://`, no trailing path. `acme.com`, not
  `https://acme.com/`.
</Note>

## Verify ownership

Expand the domain card and choose a method. You only need **one**.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="File (easiest on Vercel/Next.js)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Copy the token">
        chifu shows a token and the target URL: `https://yourdomain.com/chifu-token.txt`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create the file">
        Add `public/chifu-token.txt` to your project containing **only** the
        token, one line, nothing else.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Redeploy">
        Push and let Vercel (or your host) deploy. Confirm the file loads at the URL
        above.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Verify">
        chifu fetches the file and flips the domain to **Verified**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="DNS TXT">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your DNS provider">
        Wherever your domain's DNS lives (Cloudflare, Namecheap, your registrar…).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add a TXT record">
        - **Host / Name:** `_chifu.yourdomain.com`
        - **Value:** the `chifu-verify=…` string chifu shows you
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait, then Verify">
        Give DNS a minute to propagate, then click **Verify**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  Need to start over? **Regenerate token** issues a fresh token and invalidates the old
  one. Useful if a token leaked or you're switching methods.
</Tip>

## Subdomains

Once a root domain is verified, you can add subdomains under it (like
`api.myapp.com` or `staging.myapp.com`) with **Add subdomain**. Each subdomain verifies
the same way and can be scanned independently.

## Verified domains unlock

A green **Verified** badge means you can now:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Run scans" icon="magnifying-glass" href="/scans">
    The domain appears in the **Run scan now** dropdown.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Schedule scans" icon="calendar" href="/schedule">
    chifu adds it to your automatic scan schedule.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bypass your firewall" icon="shield" href="/firewall-bypass">
    Generate a secret header so bot protection doesn't block the scanner.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add subdomains" icon="sitemap">
    Bring more of your surface into scope.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Removing a domain

Hover a domain row and click the **✕** to remove it. This deletes the domain (and stops
it being scannable); your past scan history stays intact.
