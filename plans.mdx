The CLI is open source. Using it requires an account — every `chifu check` authenticates against your dashboard.

Plans meter **checks per month**. One run = one check, whether by hand, agent, or CI.

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Checks / month |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | $0 | 100 |
| Pro | $7.99 / mo | 400 |
| Max | $23.99 / mo | 1,600 |

Every account starts on Free. Upgrade anytime from the [dashboard](https://marshell.dev/dashboard/billing) or [marshell.dev/plans](https://marshell.dev/plans).

## What counts as a check

Each `chifu check` — local, agent-driven, or CI — counts as one check against your monthly limit.

100/month on Free covers everyday agent use. Upgrade when you check across many repos or run CI on every push.

## Sign in to start

```bash
chifu login
```

Or run the [wizard](/quickstart) — it signs you in during setup.
