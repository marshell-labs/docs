---
title: "Scheduled scans"
description: "Your scans run on autopilot. chifu picks the times, you pick the domain."
---

You don't set up cron jobs or pick times. Every account gets **automatic
scheduled scans**: chifu spreads your plan's scans across the month and runs
them for you, so there's always a fresh report waiting.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify a domain">
    As soon as you have a [verified domain](/domains), chifu starts scheduling
    scans for it, no extra step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="chifu allocates your slots">
    It places your plan's scans on low-traffic hours, load-balanced across every
    account so no single hour gets overloaded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scans run automatically">
    Each one lands in your [Scan history](/scans) like a manual scan. If you've
    connected a [Discord webhook](/alerts), you get pinged with the result.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Upcoming scans list

The **Schedule** page is a read-only **Upcoming scans** list, sorted by date. It
shows you when your next scans will run. There's no weekly grid to drag and no
times to set, those are chosen for you.

## What you control

The one thing you choose is the target. A **domain selector** sits at the top of
the page: pick a verified domain and every upcoming scan switches to it. One
shared domain, watched on a steady cadence.

<Note>
  Times are picked for you and stay fixed. Running a manual scan won't shift your
  scheduled ones, and they don't move around when you scan by hand.
</Note>

## How often scans run

Your plan sets the cadence. chifu fits the scans to your monthly quota.

| Plan | Cadence | Scans / month |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | 3 spread across the month | 3 |
| **Pro** | ~1 per day | 30 |
| **Max** | ~4 per day | 120 |

Changing your plan **recomputes the allocation automatically**, so your upcoming
list updates to match your new cadence right away. Scheduled scans count against
the same monthly quota as manual ones.

## Get notified

So you don't have to watch the dashboard, connect a **Discord webhook** on the
[Alerts](/alerts) page. chifu posts every completed scan and any critical finding
straight to your channel.

## Inactivity

Scheduling is meant for active projects. If nobody on your account signs in for
**over a month**, chifu suspends the account and cancels its scheduled scans to
avoid scanning abandoned sites. Just sign back in and your schedule is restored
automatically.
