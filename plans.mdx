The `chifu` CLI is free and open source. Running `chifu check` locally, without
an account, is unlimited and always will be.

Plans meter **synced checks**: the checks that save to your
[dashboard](/dashboard) while you are signed in. Local checks never count.

## Pricing

|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 100 |

## What counts as a synced check

A synced check is one `chifu check` run while signed in, which uploads your
dependency list to your dashboard for history and tracking.

- Running `chifu check` with no account: **free and unlimited**, never counted.
- Running `chifu check` signed in: counts as one synced check.

If you only want local checks in your editor and CI, you never need a paid plan.
