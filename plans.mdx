## What the quota means

Every scan, manual or [scheduled](/schedule), counts against your monthly quota.
The **Overview** page and the **Scans remaining** card show how many you have
left and when they reset.

| Plan | Scans / month | Roughly |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | 3 | a few spot checks |
| **Pro** | 30 | ~1 per day |
| **Max** | 120 | ~4 per day |

Quotas are per calendar month and reset at the start of each month. **Unused
scans don't roll over**, they expire when the month resets. Changing your plan
recomputes your [scheduled scan](/schedule) cadence automatically.

## Managing your plan

Your current plan and usage show on **Settings** (the Account page) and on the
**Overview** page. There's nothing to configure on Free: you're ready to scan as
soon as you verify a domain.
