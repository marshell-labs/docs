title: "Quickstart"
description: "Connect a site and start getting security alerts in under five minutes."
---

The whole point: set this up once, then let chifu watch your site and ping you
when something needs attention. No code changes, no agent to install.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [marshell.dev/register](https://marshell.dev/register) with email or
    GitHub / Google. You start on the **Free** plan: 3 scans a month, no credit card.

    <Tip>
      Don't see the dashboard? Check your inbox and click the confirmation link first.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a domain">
    Open **Domains → Add domain** and enter your hostname, `myapp.com` or
    `myapp.vercel.app`. It shows as **Pending** until you verify it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify you own it">
    Expand the domain and pick whichever is easier:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="File (easiest on Vercel)">
        Add `public/chifu-token.txt` containing only the token chifu shows, then
        redeploy. Click **Verify**.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="DNS TXT">
        Add a `TXT` record at `_chifu.yourdomain.com` with the `chifu-verify=…`
        value. Wait a minute, then click **Verify**.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    Once it goes **Verified** (green), chifu starts watching it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Turn on alerts">
    So you actually hear about new findings, open the **Alerts** page, paste your
    Discord webhook URL, and click **Save**. chifu will post completed scans and
    anything serious straight to your channel. Full guide: [Alerts](/alerts).
    (Telegram bot coming soon.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're monitored">
    That's it. chifu now [scans automatically on a schedule](/schedule). Want to
    see results right away? Hit **Scans → Run scan now** and pick your domain. The
    first report lands in seconds for most static sites.
  </Step>
</Steps>
