You shipped a site with Lovable, v0, Bolt, or Cursor. It works. But new
vulnerabilities drop every week for the stacks AI loves: Next.js, React, the
dependencies in your `package.json`. You won't hear about them. **chifu does.**

chifu is **continuous security monitoring for vibe-coded sites**. Connect your
site once and it keeps watching. When a new CVE hits your stack, a header
regresses, or something gets exposed, chifu tells you in plain English, with the
fix.

## How it works, in one breath

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your site">
    Add and verify a domain. Takes a couple of minutes, once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="chifu watches it">
    Automatic scans on a schedule, you don't run anything. It tracks your stack
    and matches known vulnerabilities against it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You get pinged">
    When something new shows up, chifu alerts you on **Discord** (Telegram coming
    soon) with what's wrong and how to fix it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fix it fast">
    Read the plain-English fix, or let your AI editor apply it for you with the
    [chifu MCP](/ai-editor), no copy-pasting reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What chifu watches for

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="New known vulnerabilities" icon="shield-halved">
    Detects your framework and dependency versions and flags the moment a
    published CVE affects them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Missing security headers" icon="lock">
    `Content-Security-Policy`, `X-Frame-Options`, HSTS, the headers AI
    scaffolding skips.
  </Card>
  <Card title="TLS & certificates" icon="certificate">
    Certificate validity, protocol version, cipher strength.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exposure & leaks" icon="eye">
    Exposed secrets, server banners, and info that shouldn't be public.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who it's for

chifu is built for **vibe-coders**: founders and builders who ship real products
with AI tools but never learned application security. If you can read English,
you can act on a chifu alert. No dashboards to babysit, no security degree
required.
