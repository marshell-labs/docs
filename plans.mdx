The `chifu` CLI is free and open source, but using it needs an account: every
`chifu check` authenticates and runs against your [dashboard](/dashboard).

Plans meter **checks per month** — each `chifu check` run counts as one,
whether you run it by hand, through the agent skill, or in CI.

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Checks / month |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 100 |
| **Pro** | $7.99 / mo | 400 |
| **Max** | $23.99 / mo | 1,600 |

## What counts as a check

chifu needs an account, so every `chifu check` runs signed in and counts as one
check against your monthly limit — whether you run it by hand, through the agent
skill in your editor, or in CI.

The free 100/month covers everyday use; upgrade when your agent checks a lot.
